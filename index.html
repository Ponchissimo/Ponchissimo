What is Gamebridge?

GAMEBRIDGE was born from the idea of helping small/medium local business reconnect with their local communities of gamers by facilitating and speeding up the investment process in esports by removing most of the hassles involved.

Through our platform businesses can easily define their branding strategy while players can join hundreds different events sponsored by them, earning prizes just by playing as they have always done. We bear the operative aspects of event management making the game easier for both sides.
